Understanding the Standard Error of a Regression Coefficient

Standard Error (se) = 2.00

The "Rotate" button animates the regression line to show the 95% confidence interval for the slope:

  1. First, the line rotates up to a slope of 1+2*se
  2. Then, it rotates down to a slope of 1-2*se
  3. Finally, it returns to the original slope of 1

This visualizes the range of plausible values for the regression coefficient.
